Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of artificial intelligence focused on building algorithms that improve automatically through experience and data analysis. Rather than being explicitly programmed, machine-learning models are trained on large datasets to find statistical relationships and make predictions. Deep Learning is a specialized subfield of ML that utilizes deep neural networks with multiple hidden layers. These neural architectures are inspired by the human brain and excel at processing high-dimensional data, such as images, audio, and large-scale text databases.
