Natural Language Processing (NLP) is an interdisciplinary field at the intersection of computer science, linguistics, and artificial intelligence. NLP aims to enable computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language in a useful way. Popular applications include translation, sentiment analysis, named entity recognition, and chatbots. Text preprocessing is a fundamental step in NLP, involving tokenization, lowercasing, stop-word removal, and stemming or lemmatization. These techniques clean raw text data, making it suitable for training machine learning algorithms and language models.
