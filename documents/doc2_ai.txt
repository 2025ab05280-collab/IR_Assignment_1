Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence by computer systems and machines. The history of AI spans several decades, starting with Alan Turing's question, 'Can machines think?' Early AI research focused on symbolic logic, expert systems, and rule-based decision engines. Over time, the field shifted towards statistical methods and machine-learning algorithms that learn patterns from data. Today, generative AI and deep neural networks are driving rapid advancements in computer vision, robotics, and automated systems, transforming how society interacts with technology.
